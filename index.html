üÜò Emergency & Safety Features

SOS Alerts: When you trigger an SOS alert, we collect and share your precise location with your designated emergency contacts via SMS. This data is stored securely and cached locally on your device if you are offline, then sent automatically when connectivity is restored.

Emergency Contacts: You may add emergency contacts who can be notified in case of emergency. Their contact information is stored securely on our servers and cached locally on your device for offline access. Cached contacts are synced to the server when you reconnect.

üîî Push Notifications

We send push notifications for trail alerts, weather warnings, and hiking milestones. You can control which notifications you receive in Settings. Weather alerts may bypass quiet hours for your safety. We use Expo Push Notification service to deliver notifications to your device.

Weather Alerts: We check the National Weather Service (NWS) API every 30 minutes for active weather alerts in the Estes Park area. When a new alert is found, we send a push notification to all users with weather alerts enabled. We do not share your data with NWS.

üåê Language & Translation

When you select a language preference, this preference is synced to our server so we can deliver notifications in your chosen language. Alert content is translated using Google Cloud Translation API. Your language preference is the only data shared with Google for this purpose.
